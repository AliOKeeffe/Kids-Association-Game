How to Play

Can you match all of the animals to their preferred habitat?

  1. To begin, choose your level of difficulty - Easy Peasy or Super Duper Hard
  2. Select your preferred habitat - Farm, Jungle, Safari or Sea
  3. Click on the animals that belong in that habitat and be quick because the clock is ticking!
  4. When you have selected all of the correct animals the game will end and you will see your score
