How to Play

The objective of the game is to match all of the animals to their preferred habitat

  1. Click on the Play button to start the game
  2. Choose your level of difficulty - Easy Peasy or Super Duper Hard
  3. Select your preferred habitat - Farm, Jungle, Safari or Sea
  4. Click on the animals that belong in that habitat. The easy level has three correct animals and one incorrect animal. The difficult level has 5 correct animals and 3 incorrect animals.
  5. When you have selected all of the correct animals the game will end and you will see your score
  6. Click on the home button at any time to return to the home screen and restart the game
